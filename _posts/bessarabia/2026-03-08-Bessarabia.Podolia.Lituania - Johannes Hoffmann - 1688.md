#### Title ####
Bessarabia.Podolia.Lituania.

#### Description ####
Small map of Bessarabia, together with Podolia and  Lituania.  In Bessarabia are shown thow cammils wach with one covered cart. As villages or city are: Tekin (Bender), Bialigrod (Cetatea Alba), Moncastro (Chilia).

Source: 
Die Donau, der Fürst aller Europaeischen Flüsse: Das ist: Eine genaue Darstellung Aller der um an der Donau gelegenen Königreiche, Fürtenthümer, Länder und Städte, beneben einer kurtzen Beschreibung selber. Derne Zu de Lesern Behuff, annoch ein Register aller der jenigen Oerter, so in denen Charten mit begrifffen seynd, beigefüget worden.
Translation: 
The Danube, the Prince of all European Rivers: That is: A precise depiction of all the kingdoms, principalities, countries, and cities situated along the Danube, along with a brief description of themselves. For the benefit of the reader, a register of all those places included in the maps has also been added.

#### Date ####
1688 ( undated )

#### Dimension ####
Paper size approx.: cm 12.5 x 14

#### Mapmaker: ####
**Johann Hoffmann** (1629-1698) was a German engraver and publisher active in Nürnberg.
