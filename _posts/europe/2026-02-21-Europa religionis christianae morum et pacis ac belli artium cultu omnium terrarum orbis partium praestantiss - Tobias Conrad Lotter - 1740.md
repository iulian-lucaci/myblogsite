#### Title ####
Europa religionis christianae morum et pacis ac belli artium cultu omnium terrarum orbis partium praestantiss - Tobias Conrad Lotter - 1740

#### Description ####
Title translation: "Europe, the most excellent of all parts of the world by its cultivation of the Christian religion, good morals, and the arts of peace and war." 
Map of Europe originally created by Matthäus Seutter (c. 1730) and later republished by his son-in-law, Tobias Conrad Lotter in his Atlas Minor at Augsbrurg, which promotes Europe as a superior civilizing force defined by enlightenment ideals, faith, and military or cultural progress.

#### Date ####
Circa 1740–1744 ( undated )

#### Dimension ####
Approximately 19.5 × 26 cm

#### Condition ####
Torn, damaged, incomplete, stained, circled, hand-colored copperplate engraving, later 1840-1860, laminated on paper.

#### Author: ####
Tobias Conrad Lotter (1717 - 1777) was a German engraver and map publisher.
