#### Title ####
Rohbock - Praetorium,  Mehadia - 1864

#### Description ####
Old lithography signed by Ludwig Rohbock and included in "Ungarn und Siebenbürgen in malerischen Original-Ansichten", publisher Gustav Georg Lange, Darmstadt 1863-1864 with texts by János Hunfalvy.
The engraving captures the Praetorium Castrum near the Cerna Valley and the Roman road leading toward the famous thermal baths Bad Mehadia / Baile Herculane, which now is an UNESCO World Heritage Site beeing part of Frontiers of the Roman Empire in Dacia.

#### Date ####
1864 ( undated )

#### Dimension ####
Picture size approx.: 11 x 16 cm 

#### Author: ####
Ludwig Rohbock (1824–1893) was a German artist, renowed for his detailed architectural and landscape depictions across Central Europe.
